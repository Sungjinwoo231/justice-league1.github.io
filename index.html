About


Fueled by his restored faith in humanity and inspired by Superman’s selfless act, Bruce Wayne enlists the
help of his newfound ally, Diana Prince, to face an even greater enemy. Together, Batman and Wonder Woman
work quickly to find and recruit a team of metahumans to stand against this newly awakened threat. But
despite the formation of this unprecedented league of heroes—Batman, Wonder Woman, Aquaman, Cyborg and
The Flash—it may already be too late to save the planet from an assault of catastrophic proportions.
